FORMULA ONE

1997 V10 Benneton F1 car

The Benetton B197 was a Formula One racing car used by the Benetton team during the 1997 Formula One season. Designed by Nick Wirth and under the technical guidance of Pat Symonds, this car featured a 3.0-liter V10 engine supplied by Renault, named the RS9.
